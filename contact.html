Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Orders within Pakistan are delivered within 3-5 business days. International orders typically take 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Custom or made-to-order items cannot be returned.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship worldwide from Islamabad, Pakistan. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! We offer customization services for most items. Contact us with your requirements, and our team will assist you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and mobile payment methods for your convenience.
